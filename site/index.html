Before you call

Common questions, answered honestly.

Is your coverage more expensive than the discount carriers?

Sometimes, yes — and that's by design. We write for real protection, not state minimums. The savings discount carriers offer typically come from lower coverage limits, higher deductibles, and less responsive claims service. For most families with assets, the math works out in favor of fuller coverage. We'll show you the difference side-by-side and you decide.

What happens after I submit the form?

You'll get a call from Candice — the actual agent, not a call center — within one business hour during office hours. The first call usually takes 5–10 minutes. No follow-up barrage. No transfer to a sales floor. If we're not a fit for your situation, we'll tell you.

Do you only do auto insurance?

No. We write auto, home, condo, renters, life, and umbrella policies, plus small business coverage. Most of our clients bundle multiple lines because the discounts are real and the convenience of one agent for everything is hard to beat.

What's umbrella insurance and do I need it?

An umbrella policy extends your liability coverage above and beyond your auto and home policy limits — typically by $1 million or more. If you have home equity, retirement savings, college funds, a business, or rental property, you almost certainly need it. It costs about $25/month for most households. We'll tell you honestly whether it makes sense for you.

How long have you been in business?

13 years. Same office in California. Same phone number. Same email. We're not going anywhere — and that matters when you're the person who'll be handling your claim in five years.

How do you handle teen drivers on a policy?

Carefully. Adding a teen significantly increases premium because the risk is real — but there are good-student discounts, telematics options, and policy structures that can soften the impact. More importantly, we'll make sure your liability limits and umbrella coverage are appropriate before that teen ever gets in the car. That's the conversation we want to have with you.
